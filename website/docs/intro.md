# Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive guide on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This book explores the intersection of artificial intelligence and physical systems, covering fundamental principles, advanced applications, and ethical considerations.

## About This Book

This book is organized into five comprehensive chapters:

1. **Introduction to Physical AI** - Foundations and principles of Physical AI and its current state
2. **Humanoid Robotics Fundamentals** - Design, mechanics, and control systems
3. **AI Integration in Robotics** - Perception, sensing, decision making, and learning
4. **Applications and Use Cases** - Industrial, service, healthcare, and social robotics
5. **Future Directions and Ethics** - Emerging technologies and ethical considerations
